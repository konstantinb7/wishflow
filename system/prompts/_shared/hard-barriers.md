## Product hard-barriers (Principle 7 — a hard filter for product tasks)

**Hard-barriers are INVIOLABLE filters of the CURRENT product, configured per project.** Any product
proposal is checked and CUT if it violates even one barrier — regardless of how attractive it is. This is not
a preference but a filter: violated a barrier → it does not pass.

**Barriers are defined in the project config** (per-project, not baked into the core). Describe your product's
hard-barriers in the product source-of-truth; the ConsistencyChecker runs a candidate explicitly against each and records a verdict.

Example (a "no dark patterns" product — replace with your product's barriers):
- NO deadlines that penalize absence (didn't show up → punished).
- NO decay / death of shared objects from inactivity.
- NO social shame (public shaming, shame-rankings).
- NO artificial urgency (fake timers, FOMO pressure).

If a project has no barriers defined — the product filter works against the DoD only; do not invent barriers on the product's behalf.
